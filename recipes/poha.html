Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flattened rice (poha)
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • A few curry leaves
  • 1 finely chopped onion
  • 1-2 green chilies, sliced
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • A handful of roasted peanuts
  • Freshly chopped coriander leaves
  • A few drops of lemon juice

Steps:

  1. Preparation:
    • Rinse 1 cup of flattened rice (poha) in water and let it sit for a few minutes to soften.
    • Drain the water completely.
  2. Tempering:
    • Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a pan.
    • Add 1 teaspoon of mustard seeds and let them splutter.
    • Add 1 teaspoon of cumin seeds, a pinch of asafoetida (hing), and a few curry leaves.
    • Add 1 finely chopped onion, 1-2 green chilies (sliced), and saut√© until the onions turn translucent.
  3. Cooking:
    • Add 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder, salt to taste, and mix well.
    • Add the softened poha and mix thoroughly to ensure it is evenly coated with the spices and onions.
  4. Final Touch:
    • Add a handful of roasted peanuts and mix well.
    • Cover the pan and let it cook on low heat for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Garnishing:
    • Turn off the heat and add freshly chopped coriander leaves and a few drops of lemon juice.
    • Mix well and serve hot.
